# Company Policies

## Remote Work Policy
Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 4 days per week with manager approval.

## Vacation and Leave
- 20 paid vacation days per year
- 10 sick days
- Paid parental leave: 12 weeks

## Confidentiality
All employees must maintain strict confidentiality of client and company data.

## Code of Conduct
Treat all colleagues with respect. No discrimination or harassment.

## IT Security
Use company VPN for remote access. Do not share passwords.
